DATs_In

INTERNAL TRANSMISSION PROTOCOL
DATs_In is the internal program hosted inside MEAI Studio.

It is a controlled environment for experimentation, testing, rehearsals, research, workshops, and intimate showings.

Within this enclosed space, ideas are refined, systems are built, and audiovisual practices are developed before expanding outward.

DATs_In functions as a laboratory — a place for process, dialogue, and structural exploration.
Location: MEAI Studio, Denpasar
Format: Workshop / Research / Studio Session / Closed Performance
